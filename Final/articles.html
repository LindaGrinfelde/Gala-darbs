Latvia

You might wonder about it, but it does work. Latvia attracts, and strongly. Physicists define magnetism as the capacity to attract. For everyone else, magnetism manifests as the desire to have a link with Latvia, whether that be through business, culture, recreation, entertainment, or personal relationships. What’s more, Latvia is also able to connect seemingly polar opposites.

People

We don’t have a huge population, but there is no shortage of talent. Not so many orators, but many movers and shakers. No great mountains, but great minds. Guests and local entrepreneurs are highly welcome to use this centre as an open office or a meeting place before or after flights.

Culture

Latvia is known for world-renowned musicians and equally for renowned quantum physicists. Latvians may be hesitant huggers, but they never shy away from hard work. We have both the oldest oak trees and the latest technology.
