Deep Tech Atelier

The Deep Tech Atelier is a conference and a practical workshop dedicated to deep tech startup creation and further development. This entrepreneurship conference is a not only a place for discussion for all interested parties – entrepreneurs, scientists and technology developers, industry representatives, investors, government and public organization. This is a practical, hands-on workshop type conference with tangible outcomes – creation of up to ten entrepreneurial science based startups based on the Commercialization Reactor’s platform.

Deep Tech Atelier will focus on health tech, med tech, space tech, AI, machine learning and advanced materials in 2020.

Export and Innovation Award

Every year, the Investment and Development Agency of Latvia (LIAA) and the Ministry of Economics organize the annual Export and Innovation Award, celebrating the leading exporters and most innovative companies in Latvia. The goal of award is to promote the competitiveness of companies in local and foreign markets, to foster growth in the quality and quantity of exports, to generate products with high added value, and to further innovation by evaluating results and identifying successful partnerships and products.

The evaluation of applicants is held in two rounds, including visits to the companies and presentations about the products or services nominated for each award.

Applicants in the category of Leading Exporter (Exporter of the Year) are nominated by a special jury, based on information about a company’s export activity and strategy for sustainability development.

Tourism Forum

The tourism industry in Latvia includes a variety of destinations, products and services, and their marketing opportunities are just as diverse. There are parties with seemingly unlimited capacity and considerable resources which unfortunately do not always achieve the best results. At the same time, there are small but important industry players who need to make greater use of creativity, alternative marketing tools, and experimentation to achieve results because resources are scarce or non-existent. The challenges of how to build a better image, how to use technical and practical innovation in branding and marketing, and how to reach your audience in a productive way have been faced by everyone in the tourism industry.

The Latvian Tourism Forum in 2019 will highlight these opposites and try to encourage, inspire, educate and encourage them to work with examples to find the answer to the question - how to present your product when money is lacking and how to do it when there is enough money?
